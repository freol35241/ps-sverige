# 2026-06-14

## Web-publication renders of the five article figures + one new

Built `src/web_figures.py` to produce web-ready versions of all five embedded
figures plus a new sixth: `21_sd_reservation_collapse.png`. Outputs land in
`figures/web/` and are copied to `site/public/figures/` so the existing
`<img>` tags in `index.astro` resolve unchanged.

Design choices (applies to all):

- 1184–1284 px wide, sized for ~720 px container with retina headroom.
- Background `#FAFAFA` everywhere; sans-serif (Inter → Helvetica → DejaVu
  fallback); base font 12 pt, axis labels 11+, titles 14 bold.
- No top/right spines, light grey grid only on Y where it helps reading.
- Tick marks length 0 (just labels), reducing visual noise.

Per-figure simplifications:

- **01 behavioural map**: dropped convex hulls and the n-count legend. Each
  party's centroid badge is the legend. Nudged KD label −3 in PC2 because
  KD, M and L coincide so closely the badges collided; added a thin
  connector line so the offset is legible. KD voting almost identically to
  M is a real finding the figure now shows rather than hides.
- **07 Tidö collapse**: same 4-panel layout, but the mean pairwise distance
  is now a prominent box (`d = 0.43` in burnt-orange on the last panel)
  rather than a small subtitle. Removed the overall convex hulls.
- **08 agreement trend**: panel B previously showed SD↔M, SD↔L, C↔S. The
  SD↔L line is fully redundant (all cabinet parties' agreement with SD is
  identical to within a percent in every year — SD votes with the whole
  cabinet). Replaced with V↔MP and S↔V opposition pairs in grey, which
  show the absolute scale of the SD jump: opposition pairs sit at 40–55%
  through the whole mandate while SD↔cabinet climbs 67→98%.
- **15 pair scatter**: kept all 28 pairs but de-emphasised them to light
  grey-blue dots. The three story pairs (S–C, M–SD, V–MP) are now ringed
  in coloured circles with bold labels.
- **16 three-axis parallel coords**: removed the right-hand "fragmented
  pairs" bar chart. The parallel-coord story stands on its own; the bar
  chart was a second story crammed in.

New:

- **21 SD reservation collapse**: line per party across the four
  riksmöten, counting reservations *signed* by the party (multi-party
  reservations counted for each signer). SD line in thick yellow, others
  in muted party colors. Annotated SD's collapse 749 → 402 → 574 → **17**.
  Opposition lines all rise or hold (V 533 → 851, MP 658 → 936, S 481 →
  813). Cabinet parties write ≤5/year and are excluded from the figure to
  avoid visual clutter at zero. Election year is shaded.
